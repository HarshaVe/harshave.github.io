About

Harsha, who is the “Harsha” and the “V” part of HarshaV.com, is currently the head of design for Robert Bosch North America. In this capacity, he is working to build on Bosch's excellence as a leading global supplier of technology and services by driving adoption of innovative, customer-driven, design-thinking solutions and services.

Before joining Robert Bosch, Harsha was the Global Connected Customer Experience team's User Experience Manager at General Motors (GM). He lead web, device, wearable experience design and overall testing efforts, focused on developing extraordinary designers and delivering remarkable designs.

Prior to joining GM, Harsha contracted to the United States Air Force's Air Mobility Command (USAF AMC), leading the design and development of a logistics software solution, as well as command-wide experience and style guidelines.

Harsha's agency and contracting experience started before the USAF when he served as Analytics and User Experience Manager at the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention. There he led Analytics and UX efforts across four divisions in building and supporting mission critical, award-winning sites and applications.

Harsha has also worked in a variety of UX, Product Management and development roles in across industries such as non-profit, education, news/media, and energy trading.

Like the Ultimate Warrior, Harsha hails from parts unknown. He would also prefer to be introduced as "weight unknown" and "height unknown."

Harsha's complete resume, recommendations, and a particularly tragic photo are available on LinkedIn, and finally, you can contact him right here.
